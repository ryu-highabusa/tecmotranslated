1:FOOTBALL FIGHTER

【Hardware】 TECMO 16bit SYSTEM BOARD
【Release】 Unreleased
【Game Intro】

TEAM NINJA's first 3D game. A project that blended elements of fighting games with American football. Although it was developed targeting the American market, it was never released due to various circumstances.

By the end of development, research on the arcade version of DEAD OR ALIVE was already underway, and the knowledge gained from this project would later contribute to the DOA series, making it a significant milestone.

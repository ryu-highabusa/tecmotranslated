Development Story

Part 1 FOOTBALL FIGHTER


Ichigo Here we introduce the glorious works of TEAM NINJA!
But, are we discussing a canceled project to start off?
EGAWA Yeah, it was canceled.
ITAGAKI It was a total loss. All the other games we were working on
at the time were released, though.
"Tokidensho" and "Ganbare Ginkun" were a few.
Miruku Huh...? Why was this canceled?
ITAGAKI It was supposed to be an American football game,
but we added punch and kick buttons,
so that probably did it.
Ichigo Woah?! You could punch someone during a football match?
ITAGAKI That's right.
EGAWA We got a bit carried away and added moves like
Punch Punch Somersault and Shoryuken.
Check out the instruction cards available then.
ITAGAKI Yup. We ended up ignoring the rules of American football
altogether. When playing defense, you'd push back the
ball carrier with punches and kicks until they were KO'd,
and the ball would be dead at the spot where they fell.
We got scolded pretty badly by the Americans
because of that. They told us, "The rules are wrong!"
Ichigo Scolded ya, huh?
OH MY GOD! That’s just so American, isn’t it?
Just kidding! Hahaha!!
ITAGAKI (ignores that)
Well, one positive is that we were the first in our company to
use 3D CGI. Egawa was the chief CGI artist at the time.
EGAWA It almost killed me! I had only used a digitizer before,
but suddenly, we had to use the SGI Indigo 2.
Ichigo Digitizer? S...?
Miruku S.G.I.???
ITAGAKI Not knowing what a digitizer is makes sense, but
you should probably have heard of SGI.
Ichigo Ugh... this is bad...
Miruku Come on, don’t feel so bad!
ITAGAKI You too!
EGAWA (whispers) I'm talking about the blue computer that
uses Softimage.
ITAGAKI But the game board was just a normal 2D board.
We rendered all the animations and stored them
as individual frames, which ended up using
an enormous amount of memory. We used
about 36 frames just for the running animation.
Miruku Ho, ho, ho. (Actually, I don't know what that means.)
EGAWA 今考えると、大ばかものでしたね。
ITAGAKI もう忘れたけど、たしか４階建ての基板に、キャラ用の
ＥＰＲＯＭを３００個くらい載せてた気がする。
Ichigo え！？　４階建て？　何か豪華デス！
ところでＥＰＲＯＭ３００個ってすごいことなんデスか？
ITAGAKI ・・・・・・。
EGAWA あの、３００個でしたっけ？
・・・ちょっと、言い過ぎじゃないですか？
ITAGAKI いいじゃん、別に。俺ももう忘れてるくらいだから。
まあとにかく、ＲＯＭが多すぎて電圧が足りなくて、
基板がブートしないというしゃれにならない時もあったな。
Ichigo なんだか、知的な話でいちごはついていけません。
Miruku まあ、そう落ち込むなって！
分からないのは君だけじゃないんだから！
Ichigo え？　まさかＹＯＵ・・・。
ITAGAKI ・・・ま、まあ、今度遊んでみてよ。
アストロに繋いであげるから、そんときに画面写真もとって、
ここにアップしよう。
Ichigo＆Miruku わっかりましたー！
EGAWA 当時、神楽坂あたりのテクモのロケでロケテストやってたから、
もしかしたらプレイしてくれた人がいるかもしれないですね。
ITAGAKI もしいたら、掲示板のほうに書いてくれるとうれしいね。
Miruku 意外と人気があって、企画復活とか。
ITAGAKI&EGAWA ないない(^^;
Miruku ですよね～。はははは！！
ITAGAKI&EGAWA ガクッ。
　
（ルール注） 本当のアメフトは、ボールキャリアの自主的後退に
よらない限り、いったん前進してゲインしたヤードは
失わないスポーツなのです。
もちろん、パンチやキックも禁止です

【Game Intro】
DEAD OR ALIVE's debut installment. Where the legend began.

Eight charismatic fighters clash across stunning environments, powered by cutting-edge arcade hardware. This revolutionary fighter introduced: The first dedicated "HOLD" button in fighting games and a groundbreaking "triangle" battle system. Beyond its famed breast physics for female characters and authentically animated martial arts motions, this game overflowed with so much innovation that words barely suffice—qualities that still command player devotion today."

The foundation for every DEAD OR ALIVE game that followed.

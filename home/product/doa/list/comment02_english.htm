Behind-the-Scenes

Part 2 DEAD OR ALIVE (MODEL2)

Ichigo Today is the Star Festival!
Miruku So we're having two "stars" reminisce developing DEAD OR ALIVE for arcades!
KAZAMAN So much happened, back then... But there's so much, that... I can't talk about.
MITSUO Haha, yeah, it was pretty insane.
Ichigo What? What? What?
KAZAMAN My lips are sealed.
Ichigo Maybe I can charm it out of you.
KAZAMAN I'm not too sure about your charm.
Miruku How about mine?
KAZAMAN Okay, I'll talk!
Ichigo What's that about???
KAZAMAN So DEAD OR ALIVE actually began as a four-player fighting game.
Ichigo Really?!
MITSUO Battles were three against one, and stage topography played a bigger part.
Ichigo So, why'd you change it?
MITSUO Well... it looked kind of lame...
KAZAMAN We made four polygonal characters, each one color: red, yellow, blue, and green.
Miruku Wait, all one color? That is lame!
MITSUO Ugh. Yeah, I decided to rewrite the whole pitch. My supervisor and I worked on it over a holiday weekend and missed the summer festivities as a result.
KAZAMAN Wasn't that pitch rejected before it was submitted?
MITSUO Ugh... After everything, we decided to go with the standard two-player fighting game format. We still wanted to mix things up and considered fighting robots at one point.
KAZAMAN That was tossed out too.
MITSUO Yeah, well, all that trial and error is how we got the current DOA!!
KAZAMAN Those were tough times. We even stayed overnight at the office.
MITSUO Working overnight makes you gain weight. I gained about 30 pounds and ended up hitting 165.
Ichigo Whoa! You got super fat!!
KAZAMAN Some women in other departments said, “Wow, Mitsuo's really packing on the weight!”
MITSUO Staying overnight meant eating lots of snacks... When I did finally get home, my mailbox was overflowing. It got so bad I was told to cancel my subscriptions.
KAZAMAN Only you could convince a salesperson to cancel a subscription instead of selling one.
MITSUO Seriously, it was so busy, hahaha.
KAZAMAN Speaking of which, we began production in September and pushed to make it to the AOU trade show in February.
MITSUO We worked up until the very last second, finally creating something we were proud of. We pulled an all-nighter, got it to the trade show in the morning, set it up, and thought, "We did a great job—hope people come try it out!"
KAZAMAN But the booth next to us was SEGA... debuting VIRTUA FIGHTER 3...
MITSUO Yeah... that’s right. Crowds gathered, but everyone was looking the other way. Nobody wanted to play our game so Gus-san and Katsu-san acted out skits to draw attention...
Ichigo Man, that’s rough...
KAZAMAN Plus, in our game... when you jumped, you'd fly like 15 meters into the air. It was more like grasshoppers fighting.
MITSUO Yup. To make matters worse... the boobs shook non-stop!
Ichigo They—what?
MITSUO So I was responsible for the "jiggle settings" but I forgot to change some flags before the show! As a result, the breasts kept shaking non-stop... and people still post about it online to this day, haha. After that, I got pulled from boob duty.
KAZAMAN Fired from boob duty!
KAZAMAN Speaking of boobs, I remember Mugen-san (the main "jiggle" programmer) was forced to code while watching Adult Videos next to his computer. “Don’t be embarrassed!” the boss would yell.
MITSUO Impossible doesn’t even begin to cover it.
Miruku&Ichigo Anyway...
Ichigo I believe this was Mitsuo's first forray into 3D gaming. How'd you learn the programming know-how?
MITSUO Well, I started from scratch, taught myself, and made a masterpiece of code. Everyone believed in me and trusted me completely.
KAZAMAN What?! That's not true!! Watching Mitsuo twist and turn three wooden chopsticks as if they were the X, Y, and Z axes while coding made everyone nervous!
MITSUO You mean "Mr. 3D"? I still use him. He's super handy.
KAZAMAN It's wild how cutting-edge technology was made with tools that look like a kid’s craft project.
MITSUO Y'know the janitor tossed "Mr. 3D" a few times. We're currently on the 8th generation.
Miruku&Ichigo Moving along!!
KAZAMAN All right, here’s the last thing. I’m going to share a game secret that no one knows about.
Ichigo Oh?! What is it? What is it? What is it?!
KAZAMAN Want to know? Should I tell...
Miruku Just tell us already.
KAZAMAN Okay, okay. So, Bayman's stage has fans running in the storage area and the wind is actually simulated with physics calculations, which causes waistbands to flutter.
MITSUO Huh? That’s a thing? No one’s noticed that, right?
KAZAMAN I just learned about this yesterday.
Ichigo That's amazing!!　 Are we allowed to share that??
Miruku Hey Ichigo! What's the big deal? If no one noticed then it's like it never existed!
Ichigo What possessed you??
THE END
